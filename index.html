About Us

Destination Key is a part of the Holiday Booking group which is a trading name of The Midcounties Co-operative Ltd and is a member of Midcounties Co-operative Travel Consortium.

Destination Key has over 22 years of experience which includes operating in the corporate and leisure industries. During this time, we have had the privilege of experiencing many destinations
within Europe, America, Canada, the Caribbean, UAE, and Asia. Both as Solo business travelers and as well as a Family.

We can provide first-hand advice on several countries and hotels including Disney. With the knowledge gained over the last 22 years, we truly believe that one type of holiday doesn’t suit all.
So we pride ourselves on being able to tailor your dream holiday for you from start to finish.

We can offer a wide variety of options including city breaks, family holidays, cruises, beach weddings, honeymoon, babymoon, luxury and family cruises
as well as many other such as attraction tickets.

We have access to over 25000 travel , all of which are covered under our ABTA and ATOL licenses, All holidays are personally sourced, Hence the absence of a bookable website.

We are firm believers in building a rapport and a relationship with our clients. This enables us find to exactly what is required for you at the right price.
